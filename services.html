Redefining Authenticity

The Nile Style is a modern barbershop with an old school vibe. Since 1998, we have served the community with a family-friendly and professional atmosphere. Our skilled barbers have years of experience and are dedicated to providing the best service possible. We specialize in classic haircuts, beard trims, and straight razor shaves, using only the finest grooming products. At The Nile Style, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our commitment to our clients. Come in and experience the perfect blend of old-fashioned barbering and contemporary style.Nile Style Barbershop serves men, women, and children. We accept appointments and walk-ins. Come in and enjoy free wi-fi, flat screen TV's, mellow music, and a friendly atmosphere.
